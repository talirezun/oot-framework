# Knowledge Immortality: The Second Brain

*Upstream article published in the Curator research section. Linked here because the case for durable, portable, plain-markdown knowledge directly motivates ØØT's Brain architecture and the audit-trail discipline in R6.*

Why knowledge held in proprietary SaaS dies with the vendor, and what it takes for a firm's institutional memory to outlive any single tool, employee, or LLM generation. The argument behind ØØT's choice to keep the Brain in plain markdown under git rather than in a hosted knowledge base.
