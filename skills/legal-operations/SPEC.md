# Skill Pack S8 — Legal Operations: SPEC

**ID:** S8 | **Tier:** 2 | **Status:** Scaffold in v1.0; harden in v1.x

## Purpose

Harvey-style patterns for in-house legal work in an ØØT-native firm. Contract review, due diligence, partner agreement template management, dispute resolution coordination. Designed to augment a counsel-engaged firm, not replace counsel.

Contract drafting prompts. Due diligence agent patterns. Partner Charter template management. Output Spec legal review. Variable pay structure legal verification. Long-tail entitlement legal wrapper preparation. Coordination with external counsel on the eleven legal touchpoints (`docs/06-when-to-call-a-lawyer.md`).
