# References — Skill Pack S5 (Reporting & Business Review)

The intellectual foundations of the framework's operational heartbeat.

## Primary sources

1. **Hope, J. & Fraser, R.** *Beyond Budgeting: How Managers Can Break Free from the Annual Performance Trap*. Harvard Business School Press, 2003. The rolling-forecast and continuous-allocation principles the pack borrows.

2. **Cali Ressler & Jody Thompson.** *Why Work Sucks and How to Fix It*. Penguin, 2008. ROWE — the basis for the "BR replaces status meetings" discipline.

3. **Microsoft.** *Work Trend Index 2025: The Frontier Firm.* The "human-agent ratio" and KPI framing the BR's KPI block uses.

## Adjacent literature

4. **Doerr, J.** *Measure What Matters* (Portfolio, 2018). OKR framework. Cited for the discipline of explicit decision documentation, though the framework prefers decision records over OKRs.

5. **Christensen, C. M.** *The Innovator's Dilemma* (Harvard Business Review Press, 1997). For the framing of the BR as a partnership-wide attention focal point rather than a top-down review.

6. **Edmondson, A.** *The Fearless Organization* (Wiley, 2018). Psychological safety in BR meetings — informs the "Don't run BR with same facilitator >4 weeks in a row" discipline.
