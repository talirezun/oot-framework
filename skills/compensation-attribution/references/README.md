# References — Skill Pack S3 (Compensation & Attribution)

The intellectual and empirical foundations of the most distinctive ØØT pack.

## Primary practitioner sources

1. **Yolo Investments (Tim Maslyukov).** *Stop paying for hours. Start paying for output.* Yolo Investments blog, May 2026. The seven-layer compensation framing this pack operationalises (base + variable + long-tail + subscription credits + bonus + dividends + capital appreciation).

2. **MHPR Advisors.** *Reward Species Typology — the seven partnership reward models.* Industry working paper, 2023. The eat-what-you-kill / lockstep / hybrid taxonomy ØØT mandates as a written declaration.

## Academic foundations

3. **Levin, J. & Tadelis, S.** *Profit Sharing and the Role of Professional Partnerships*. Stanford Graduate School of Business working paper, 2005. The empirical case for partnership structures in human-capital-intensive production.

4. **Weitzman, M. L.** *The Share Economy: Conquering Stagflation*. Harvard University Press, 1984. The macroeconomic case for share/profit-sharing compensation.

5. **Hope, J. & Fraser, R.** *Beyond Budgeting: How Managers Can Break Free from the Annual Performance Trap*. Harvard Business School Press, 2003. The rolling-forecast and continuous-allocation principles.

6. **Ressler, C. & Thompson, J.** *Why Work Sucks and How to Fix It*. Penguin, 2008. The ROWE (Results-Only Work Environment) framework. Documents 35% productivity gains and 52–90% reductions in voluntary turnover across Best Buy, Gap, IBM case studies.

## Industry data

7. **MBO Partners.** *State of Independence in America 2025*. Industry report, 2025. Source for the 5.6M independent workers earning >$100k/year statistic and the 36% gig-economy participation figure.

8. **DORA Report 2025.** *State of AI-Assisted Software Development*. The basis for the centaur-pattern context behind the value-tier rubric (§4.10).

9. **Microsoft.** *Work Trend Index 2025: The Frontier Firm*. The "human-agent ratio" framing that informs the §4.9 anomaly checks.

## Stablecoin payroll (Gen 2 readiness)

10. **Rise (Circle partner).** $1.37B lifetime stablecoin payroll volume by Q1 2026. https://www.riseworks.io/

11. **Circle.** USDC and EURC stablecoins. https://www.circle.com/

12. **EU MiCA** (Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation) — fully in force since January 2025. Regulatory framework for stablecoin payroll in EU jurisdictions.

13. **US GENIUS Act** — signed into law 17 July 2025. First US federal stablecoin framework.
