# Solunar theses

Our working adaptation of [`MANIFESTO.md`](../../../MANIFESTO.md). We hold four of the five theses as explicit operating concerns; Thesis 5 (composability) we assume rather than articulate at 3 partners.

1. **Centaur, not autopilot.** AI authors; a partner is accountable. Every `ai-replaces-human` change passes the [[klarna-tests/KT-2026-001|Klarna Test]].
2. **Pay for output, not seat-time.** Variable pay tracks shipped value; see [[compensation/2026-04/summary]].
3. **Measure the gap.** Self-reported productivity is checked against DORA/METR-style signal — see [[change/perception-gap-2026-04]].
4. **Discipline is in the framework.** The Klarna Test can decline work. At 3 partners, the founder is the non-beneficiary reviewer only when a third partner is genuinely uninvolved.

Frame: *"if it can't work at 3, the framework is wrong."*
