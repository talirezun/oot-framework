# 04 — Running the Friday Business Review

**Audience:** Founder + partners. Anyone who'll attend or facilitate the BR.
**Time:** 30 minutes (the meeting itself). On time. Non-negotiable in length.
**You will end with:** a Ledger page committed within 30 min of meeting end with all decisions logged.

> 📖 **References:** Skill Pack [`skills/reporting-business-review/SKILL.md`](../skills/reporting-business-review/SKILL.md), Routine [`routines/cloud/R2.md`](../routines/cloud/R2.md), template [`templates/brain/business-review.md`](../templates/brain/business-review.md). The walkthrough version with screenshots is at [`docs/walkthroughs/W4-running-the-friday-business-review.md`](walkthroughs/W4-running-the-friday-business-review.md).

## What this is + the first 5 minutes

The Friday Business Review (BR) **replaces all status meetings**. It is the partnership's only standing operational meeting. Agenda is generated by Routine R2 at Friday 08:00. Meeting is at 14:00 (or whatever the firm's standing time is). Length: 30 minutes. Always.

**Roles** (rotate weekly; listed in `firm/partners/index.md`):
- **Facilitator:** keeps to time, reads agenda, calls on people.
- **Scribe:** captures decisions in real time (creates the `firm/decisions/D-YYYY-NNN.md` page during the meeting).
- **Decision-recorder:** same as scribe in small orgs; separate in larger.

The 30-min structure is non-negotiable:

| Block | Time | What |
|---|---|---|
| **Outputs** | 10 min | Top 5 from X3 column B. Brief congrats; sharp questions only on outliers. |
| **Blockers** | 5 min | Each open blocker: owner, ETA, what help is needed. |
| **Decisions** | 10 min | Work through Decisions_Due. Each decision = a Ledger page. |
| **Klarna status** | 3 min | Open tests; tests resolved this week; new triggers. |
| **KPI snapshot** | 2 min | One look at the dashboard. Comment if something moved materially. |

## Pre-meeting (Friday 09:00 onward)

**R2 fired at 08:00.** It populated:
- `business-review.xlsx` Weekly_Review row (X3).
- `firm/business-reviews/<today>-pre.md` Brain page (pre-meeting state).
- Slack `#business-review` (or 4thtech dChat) draft summary.

**Facilitator (you, this week):**
1. Open the Slack/dChat draft. Skim. Confirm with founder if anything looks off.
2. Open the X3 Weekly_Review row. Confirm the top-5 outputs make sense.
3. Open `firm/business-reviews/<today>-pre.md`. The page mirrors X3 plus has post-meeting empty sections.
4. **Pre-read all decisions due** ≥24h prior to BR (per the discipline). If any decision_due was added <24h ago, table it for next week.

---

## The meeting itself (30 min)

### 14:00 — Outputs (10 min)

Read top 5 from X3 column B. For each:
- Quick congrats / acknowledgement.
- **Sharp questions only on outliers.** Don't ask routine questions ("how was that?"). Ask only if something looks anomalous.

**Anti-pattern:** every partner narrating their week. That's the ledger's job. Outputs block is for the top 5 only.

### 14:10 — Blockers (5 min)

Per blocker (from X3 column C):
- Owner.
- ETA.
- What help is needed.

**Anti-pattern:** solving the blocker in the meeting. If a blocker needs >1 minute of discussion, it's a follow-up, not a BR item.

### 14:15 — Decisions (10 min)

Work through Decisions_Due (X3 column F). For each decision:
1. Briefly state the decision and the options pre-read suggests.
2. Discuss only the unresolved part.
3. **Decision recorded in real time** by the scribe at `firm/decisions/D-YYYY-NNN.md` per [`templates/brain/decision-record.md`](../templates/brain/decision-record.md).
4. Outcome announced.

**Anti-pattern:** one decision dominating. If a decision takes >5 minutes, it's a working session, not a BR item. Spawn a follow-up.

### 14:25 — Klarna status (3 min)

From X3 column D:
- Open tests + their state.
- Tests resolved this week.
- New triggers since last BR.

**Standing item — never skip**, even if no open tests. The absence is information.

### 14:28 — KPI snapshot (2 min)

From X3 column E:
- One look at the dashboard.
- Comment if something moved materially (>10% week-over-week on customer count, runway, ai_skill_roi).
- **Detailed analysis is not for BR.** That's the monthly rollup (S5 §4.5).

### 14:30 — Done

The facilitator closes on time. Always.

---

## Post-meeting (within 30 min of close)

The scribe (or facilitator) finalises the Ledger page:

1. Open `firm/business-reviews/<today>-pre.md`.
2. Append post-meeting content per [`templates/brain/business-review.md`](../templates/brain/business-review.md):
   - Outputs discussion notes (1-3 sentences per discussed output).
   - Blocker notes.
   - Decisions taken (each linked to its `firm/decisions/D-YYYY-NNN.md`).
   - Klarna discussion.
   - KPI discussion.
   - Action items with owners and deadlines.
3. **Rename** the page: drop `-pre` → `firm/business-reviews/<today>.md`.
4. Set frontmatter `status: active`.
5. Signed git commit.
6. Post to Slack/dChat `#business-review`:
   > {{DATE}} BR done. {{N}} decisions, {{X}} blockers resolved, {{Y}} new. Summary at [[business-reviews/{{DATE}}]].

## When BR runs long (the diagnostic)

If a BR runs >30 min more than once in a quarter, run the diagnostic at the start of the next BR (5 min added):

- **Was the agenda overstuffed?** Move excess to next week or a working session.
- **Was a decision under-prepared?** Decisions need ≥24h Brain pre-read.
- **Is BR being used as a 1-1 substitute?** Move to quarterly check-in or ad-hoc.
- **Is one partner dominating?** Facilitator addresses directly. Most common cause.

The facilitator stops the meeting if it runs long, rather than rushing through. The 30-minute discipline is the discipline.

---

## Common pitfalls

1. **Skipping the Klarna status block.** Standing item. Never skip.
2. **Founder dominating decisions.** Founder is one voice on each decision per the RACI. The scribe records all consulted partners' input.
3. **Decisions logged in chat instead of Brain.** Every BR decision = a Ledger page. Created during the meeting, not after.
4. **Same facilitator for >4 weeks.** Rotation is deliberate. The meeting belongs to the partnership.
5. **Action items without owners + deadlines.** Both are required. *"We'll figure it out"* is not an action item.

---

## When to escalate

- **A blocker has persisted >2 BRs:** at the third BR, the blocker becomes a decision-pending item; the partnership decides on path forward (per S5 §4.8).
- **A decision needs counsel input:** table to the Tier-3 escalation per `governance/DECISION-RIGHTS.md`.
- **A Klarna Test in scoring is overdue:** founder direct decision (never auto-proceed).
